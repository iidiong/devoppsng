About Us

Serving our community since 2021

Devopps Enterprise Limited is an integrated crops and livestock farm located in Ukana Ikot Akpabin, Essien Udim, Akwa Ibom, Nigeria. This innovative farm combines crop production with raising livestock, fostering a sustainable agricultural approach to create economic opportunities to improve the well-being of the community.

When you choose Devopps, you're not just choosing fresh produce; you're choosing a commitment to health, community, and sustainability. Our integrated approach to farming reflects our dedication to creating a lasting impact.

Join us in cultivating not just crops, but a healthier, more vibrant future for our community.


Our Mission

We are dedicated to producing affordable quality agricultural products year-round for our customers.


Our Vision

Our vision is to be an innovative agricultural enterprise that combines natural resources and technology to produce quality products in a sustainable way to meet the demands of our customers.


Products

Fresh Tomatoes

Our farm combines traditional farming methods with modern techniques to bring you tomatoes that are bursting with flavor and nutrition.

Our tomatoes are handpicked at the peak of ripeness to ensure they are juicy, sweet, and full of flavor.

Experience the difference of fresh, farm-grown tomatoes. Visit our farm to pick your own tomatoes straight from the vine. If you can't make it to the farm, you can conveniently order our tomatoes online and have them delivered to your doorstep.

Fresh Cucumbers

Our cucumbers are cultivated with care and dedication, ensuring they are the perfect addition to your salads, sandwiches, and snacks.

Packed with essential vitamins and minerals, our cucumbers are a healthy choice for your daily diet.

Whether you're making a refreshing salad, a crunchy snack, or a gourmet dish, our cucumbers are versatile and enhance any meal.

Fresh Garden Eggs

These vibrant vegetables, known scientifically as Solanum aethiopicum, are a variety of eggplant native to Africa.

A great source of vitamins and minerals, particularly vitamin C, potassium, and dietary fiber.

Fresh Okra

Okra is a nutritional powerhouse, packed with vitamins, minerals, and fiber. It's a versatile ingredient perfect for a variety of dishes, from traditional stews to modern cuisine.

Whether you're a seasoned chef or a home cook, our okra is perfect for creating healthy and flavorful meals.

Eggplants

We take pride in cultivating premium eggplants that embody the perfect balance of taste, quality, and sustainability.

Our eggplants are grown with the utmost care, ensuring they are a healthy and versatile addition to your kitchen.

Oyster Mushrooms

Our farm is dedicated to sustainable and organic farming practices, ensuring that each mushroom you enjoy is not only delicious but also grown with care and respect for the environment.

Our mushrooms are hand-picked at the peak of freshness, ensuring you receive only the best.

Oyster Mushrooms is delicate and tender, perfect for stir-fries, soups, and pasta dishes

Farm-Raised Catfish

We are dedicated to producing high-quality, farm-fresh eggs that are both delicious and nutritious. Our hens are raised in a healthy, natural environment, ensuring that every egg we collect is of the highest standard.
