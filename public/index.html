About Devopps


Serving our community since 2022

Devopps Enterprise Limited is an integrated farm with multiple farm units working together to address food access, empower and inspire individual, and create economic opportunities to improve the standard of life in the communities. Our diverse farm reflects our belief that a multifaceted approach is needed to address food insecurity in the 21st. century.

Our Mission


Our mission is to grow food, create economic opportunities to improve the health and well-being of our community and future generations.

Our Vision


We aspire to develop an innovative and sustainable agricultural enterprise where we produce high-quality food and products for our customers and create economic opportunities to improve the health and well-being of our community and future generations.
